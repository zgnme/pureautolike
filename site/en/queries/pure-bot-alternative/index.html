Why not a bot

The extension works where Pure Web is already open and does not replace the account or conversation with a separate bot.

What it automates

Visible likes, accessible photos, Telegram events, timers, and local export.

Honest boundary

PureAutoLike does not message people for you and does not guarantee replies.

What PureAutoLike does

  • Auto-like for visible profiles in an open Pure Web tab.
  • Opening only photos available to the current Pure session.
  • Optional Telegram alerts for matches, likes, and messages.
  • Local Markdown export of visible descriptions when enabled manually.
